Sushi Vinegar

Description

Prepared vinegar, usually with some salt and sugar used for sushi. Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish of the prepared vinegared rice and a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 798ml rice vinegar
  • 298g brown sugar
  • 149g sea salt
  • 7.1g kombu

Steps/Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to medium saucepan.
  2. Heat ingredients until sugar has just dissolved. Do not boil!
  3. Let cool. Transfer to airtight container
